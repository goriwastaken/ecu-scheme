Enforce Dirichlet boundary conditions for the convection-diffusion problem The boundary in question corresponds to the inflow boundary on the unit rectangle where the velocity field is assumed to have the form \(\vec{v} = (c, c)^T\) for some constant \(c\). Thus the inflow boundary is \(\Gamma = \Gamma_1 \cup \Gamma2 \) with \( \Gamma_1 = \{(x, y) \in [0, 1]^2 \mid x = 0 \} \), and \( \Gamma_2 = \{(x, y) \in [0, 1]^2 \mid y = 0 \} \).
